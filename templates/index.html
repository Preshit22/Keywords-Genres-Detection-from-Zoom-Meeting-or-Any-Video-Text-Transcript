About us

"Keywords Identification from Zoom Meeting or Any Video" is a sophisticated system designed to extract and analyze key terms, phrases, and topics from video content. It utilizes advanced algorithms to process a wide range of video formats, including Zoom meetings and other sources. The system offers multilingual support, ensuring the accurate identification of keywords in videos of various languages.
